Frequently Asked Questions

What makes these NFTs different from regular NFTs?

Our NFTs represent actual Real-World Assets (RWAs) - vehicles, venue access, and infrastructure. They generate revenue from real business operations and provide tangible utility beyond digital ownership.

How do revenue distributions work?

Revenue from bookings, events, and services is distributed to NFT holders based on their tier and ownership percentage. Distributions are made weekly, monthly, or quarterly depending on the NFT type.

Can I resell my NFT?

Yes! All NFTs can be traded on secondary marketplaces. Nodaluxe receives a 5-10% royalty on secondary sales, which helps fund platform development and benefits all holders.

Are these NFTs regulated securities?

NFTs that provide revenue share may be considered securities and are structured to comply with SEC regulations. Venue access and experience NFTs without revenue share are utility tokens.

What blockchain are these NFTs on?

Nodaluxe NFTs are minted on Ethereum and Polygon networks for maximum compatibility and lower transaction fees. Cross-chain bridging is supported.
